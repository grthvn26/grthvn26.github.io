Why Osaka is Special

Osaka is famous for its modern architecture, vibrant nightlife, and delicious street food. It's also home to the iconic Osaka Castle. Osaka, Japan, is known for its vibrant street food scene, historic landmarks like Osaka Castle, lively entertainment districts such as Dotonbori, and its rich cultural heritage, including traditional performing arts like Bunraku puppet theater. Additionally, it's a major economic and commercial hub in Japan.

How to Get There

Fly to Kansai International Airport, then take a train to Osaka city center.
