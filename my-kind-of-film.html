"The Crown" is a historical drama television series that chronicles the life and reign of Queen Elizabeth II, focusing on the political and personal events that shaped her rule from her wedding in 1947 to the early 21st century. The show features a rotating cast of actors, with Claire Foy and Olivia Colman portraying the Queen in earlier seasons, and Imelda Staunton taking on the role in the later seasons.

Actors:

  • Claire Foy
