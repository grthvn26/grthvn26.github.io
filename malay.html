Why Kuala Lumpur is Special

Kuala Lumpur is known for its iconic Petronas Twin Towers, bustling markets, and diverse food scene. The capital of Malaysia is widely recognized for numerous landmarks, including Petronas Twin Towers (twin skyscrapers with a sky bridge and observation deck), the Petaling Street flea market, and Batu Caves, which is over 400 million years old.

How to Get There

Fly to Kuala Lumpur International Airport, then take a train or taxi to the city center.

Other Places to Visit

  • Batu Caves
  • Bukit Bintang
  • Merdeka Square
  • Central Market
  • Thean Hou Temple
