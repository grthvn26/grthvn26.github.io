My Beats

Rap

Chase Atlantic - Phases

Phases is the third studio album by Australian alternative band Chase Atlantic, released on 7 June 2019.

Bone Thugs N Harmony - Crossroads

Crossroads is a song by American hip hop group Bone Thugs-n-Harmony. It was released in May 1996 as the third single from their second studio album, E. 1999 Eternal.

Kendrick Lamar - Luther

Luther is the 3rd track on Kendrick Lamar’s 6th studio album, and was later made the 3rd single from GNX. it marks the 5th collaboration between him and long-time close friend, SZA. The song’s name “luther” originates from the heavily sampled track “If This World Were Mine” by Luther Vandross.

Pop-Punk

Panic! At The Disco - High Hopes

High Hopes is a song by American pop rock band Panic! at the Disco. It was released on May 23, 2018, through Fueled by Ramen as the second single from their sixth studio album, Pray for the Wicked (2018).

Don’t You Know Who I Think I Am? - Fall Out Boy

“Don’t You Know Who I Think I Am” is the first song that lead singer Patrick Stump ever co-produced on behalf of Fall Out Boy.

Dear Maria, Count Me In - All Time Low

Dear Maria, Count Me In is a song by American rock band All Time Low. The song is the second single taken from their second studio album So Wrong, It's Right.

Pop

It's Not Living - The 1975

“It’s Not Living (If It’s Not with You)” is an upbeat song that depicts Matty Healy’s struggle with heroin addiction.

Break Free - Ariana Grande, ZEDD

"Break Free" is a song by American singer Ariana Grande, featuring Russian-German music producer Zedd.

Pink Pony Club - Chappell Roan

Pink Pony Club" is a song by the American singer and songwriter Chappell Roan. It was released through Atlantic Records on April 3, 2020, and later included on her debut album The Rise and Fall of a Midwest Princess (2023).
